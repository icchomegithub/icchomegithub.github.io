Inter & Intra Transfers

Inter Bank Transfer
This enables electronic transfer of funds from the account of the remitter in Street Capito to the account of the beneficiary maintained with any other Bank. There are two systems of Inter Bank Transfer - RTGS and NEFT. Both these systems are maintained by Reserve Bank(Available in India S.Africa Nigeria)

Make an Intra Bank Transfer
